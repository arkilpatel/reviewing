### Impact Assessment
**1. Technical Significance (70%):** 
The technical significance of UniRoute is substantial. As the landscape of LLMs changes rapidly with new open-source models being released weekly, a routing mechanism that does not require full retraining for every new model is highly practical. The idea of representing a model by its empirical error on a validation set is lightweight, feasible, and directly addresses the cold-start problem in model routing.

**2. Scientific Significance (30%):**
The scientific contribution lies in formalizing the dynamic routing problem and showing that error-based feature vectors for LLMs contain enough signal to effectively route among them. The excess risk bound provides a solid theoretical foundation for this empirical finding.
