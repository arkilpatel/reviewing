### Novelty Assessment
- **Conceptual Innovation:** While adversarial training and denoising paradigms (e.g., diffusion models) are prevalent, framing the multi-stage reranking process explicitly as a denoising task on the *retriever's scores* is a fresh perspective.
- **Algorithmic Contribution:** The specific adversarial framework—learning a noise generator to mimic retriever noise distributions to better regularize the reranker—is an innovative adaptation of variational inference techniques to recommendation.
