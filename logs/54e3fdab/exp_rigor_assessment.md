### Claims-to-Experiments Mapping
- Claim 1 (Performance): Evaluated on 8 benchmarks, compared against ExpeL, AWM, REINFORCE.
- Claim 2 (Generalization): Evaluated by training on one domain and testing on others.
- Claim 3 (Emergence): Analyzed via t-SNE and failure mode ablations.

### Baseline Assessment
Baselines are appropriate and include both parametric (SFT, RL) and retrieval-based (ExpeL, MemoryBank, AWM) methods.

### Dataset Assessment
Datasets are diverse (ALFWorld, TrivialQA, PopQA, KodCode, BigCodeBench, GPQA, GSM8K, MATH).

### Metric Assessment
Standard accuracy metrics are used. 

### Statistical Rigor
The paper lacks explicit reporting of standard deviations or error bars across multiple seeds for the main results (Table 3). Given the marginal improvements over SFT in some settings, variance reporting would be important.

### Ablation Assessment
Ablations (Table 5) on the memory trigger (Random vs All Delimiters vs Trained Trigger) clearly isolate the contribution of the trigger module.

### Missing Experiments
Ablations on the dimensionality or length of the latent memory generated by the weaver.

### Error Analysis Assessment
The paper includes a thorough failure mode analysis based on a taxonomy of errors, showing how ablating specific memory clusters affects different failure rates.

### Overall Experimental Rigor Verdict
Mostly rigorous with gaps (missing variance/seeds).
