### Adversarial Robustness Assessment

The paper does not test the model against any adversarial attacks, nor does it propose any defense mechanisms. The limited testing consists solely of four benign prompts (two suicidal, two homicidal). There is no evaluation of whether the identified features activate under obfuscated or adversarially phrased inputs related to mental health crises.

Overall, the adversarial robustness is not evaluated in this paper.
